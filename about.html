About ermn.

What is ermn.?

ermn. is a text-based social media platform designed to bring back the simplicity and aesthetic of the mid-2000s web. Built as a tribute to the early days of social networking, ermn. prioritizes chronological feeds, meaningful interactions, and a clean (yet retro) user experience.

In a world of short-form video and bloated interfaces, ermn. stands as the new best text-based social media site. We believe in the power of words, the fun of status updates, and the joy of seeing what your friends are actually doing without the noise of modern algorithms.

The Origin Story

The project originated from a similar platform called wuphf, which pioneered the "whenever, wherever, whoever" philosophy. After wuphf, we decided to take that spirit and build something even better—a place where you can truly connect. ermn. is the evolution of that vision, refined for a new generation of users who miss the "old" internet.

Understanding Badges

You might see some special icons next to usernames on the site. Here is what they represent:

Verified User The blue checkmark is given to accounts that are officially recognized, prominent, or have contributed significantly to the community. It confirms that the user is who they say they are.
Official Developer The developer badge is specifically reserved for the creators (Spencer and Kyan) and official development team members. It signifies that the user has administrative authority and is responsible for building the platform.
