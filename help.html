Help & FAQ

Getting Started

How do I change my profile picture?

Go to your Profile page and look for the "Update Picture" section in the sidebar. You can upload any image from your device. Square images work best!

How do I personalize my theme?

Head over to Settings. We have several retro-inspired themes like "Classic Blue," "Bubblegum," and "Midnight." Pick one and it will apply to the whole site immediately.

Features

What are polls and how do I create one?

When writing a post, click the "poll Add Poll" button. You can add up to 4 options. Once you post it, other users can vote, and you'll see the results in real-time.

What is a "Private Account"?

If you enable "Private Account" in your Settings, people will have to send you a follow request. Only approved followers will be able to see your posts.

How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?

Head to Settings and find the "Account Security" section. Click the 2FA button to display a QR code. Scan this code with an Authenticator App (like Google Authenticator or Authy), then enter the 6-digit code to complete setup.

What is the difference between "Home" and "Feed"?

Your Home feed displays posts from users you follow in chronological order. The Feed (Algorithmic) displays popular and recommended posts from the entire ermn. community, regardless of who you follow.

How do I get a Verified Badge?

Verification badges are granted by the moderators to prominent community members or recognized figures. Stay active, post great content, and you might see a blue checkmark appear next to your name!

Safety & Support

How do I report a post?

If you see something that violates our terms, click the flag Report button at the bottom of the post. This will alert our moderation team so they can take action.
